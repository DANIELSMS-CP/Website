First Audit Which I Almost Won (How I Almost Gain $100+ from a Single Contest)

June 7, 2025

Recently, I joined an audit in Cantina (https://lnkd.in/g7mNXZwB). It was a 12-day long audit contest, which has 287 nSLOC. It was a rather short contract that I thought that I would've had a chance of at least winning some money. Everyday, I read the scope of the codebase and try to think of anything that might break the contract functionality. After around 3 days (or more), I thought of something that seems unbelieveable. Two users can have the same override address, which can lead to problems, such as reward stealing in overrideReceiver. I did think about it but I was very unsure, since the hack is quite trivial. After the contest ended, I asked my good friend neko_nyaa and he said that the bug was that, but I just misjudged it (I got the bug, but the mistake was not on that aspect). I was feeling sad, but at least I learn some things:

  1. Anything can happen in real life
  2. Always have the hacker mindset
  3. Do more contest (obviously)
