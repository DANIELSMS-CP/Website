Maimai Tips

May 31, 2025

I've been playing Maimai alot to the point where I have reached 14k rating. It's a fun game where you can also make friends and look cool. You can also treat it as an exercise. I feel like people should play this, as it is a very underrated game

As shown above, I think I can give some people tips on how to play the game (at least for levels like 12+ until 13):

  • Try to lightly tap the buttons

    • Unlike others, I think that the purpose of lightly tapping buttons is just to conserve stamina. Many people say that it keeps the machine stay in good condition but I usually don't care about it (unless you are hitting it too damn loud). It will just break anyway.

  • Play stamina chart often

    • Want to play 6 credits without break? Play Love's Theme of BADASS in one credit and try to play random 14 in the next credit. If you can sustain this, I believe that you can sustain 6 credits without break in your difficulty.

  • Play overlevel (Note that this is only for someone that has enough self-awareness that they will not smash the machine. Once again I do not care about this but people might stop you from playing)

    • Playing 14+ helps you know more about standard techniques used in hard levels, enabling you to sightread easier charts but with similar techniques.

  • Watch Youtube

    • No explanation needed LOL.

  • Play Sugarholic by Suou Patra

    • Best song and a free 13.
